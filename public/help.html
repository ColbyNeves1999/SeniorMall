Welcome to SeniorMall Help

Thank you for choosing SeniorMall for managing your mall experience online. Below is a guide to help you make the most of your digital shopping experience.

Getting Started:

Upon visiting our website, you'll find essential information about the mall, including its logo, location, hours of operation, contact details, and a list of stores available. To explore further, click on any store to view its inventory and details.

Creating an Account:

For a personalized experience, consider creating an account:

  • Click on "Create Account" and enter your details to create your account.
  • Benefits include organizing favorite stores, building a cart, leaving reviews, reviewing purchase history, and receiving birthday emails.

Database Management:

Our system relies on a database structure to manage relationships between the mall, stores, and users:

  • Real-time visibility into stock levels for each store.
  • Check anticipated delivery times for new stock.
  • Store owners can log in to manage inventory.

Technological Infrastructure:

Our system utilizes various technologies to ensure smooth operation:

  • Digital Ocean server hosts our databases and the website.
  • Primary programming language: Typescript for server-side development.
  • Node.js for server-side development and management.
  • SQL for storing and managing data within the database.
  • Website appearance is constructed using HTML, CSS, and Photoshop for design.

Using the System:

Explore the entire collection of stores within the mall through our innovative web page. Stores and their inventories are stored in our backend database, ensuring accurate and up-to-date information. Different access levels are available for store owners to manage stock and orders. Customers can create accounts with various benefits for a more personalized shopping experience.

If you need further assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us.
